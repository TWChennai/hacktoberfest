About

An open event for people to come and contribute to Open Source as part of Hacktoberfest

Hacktoberfest is a month-long celebration of open source software run by DigitalOcean and Dev. It happens in the month of October every year. ThoughtWorks Chennai is hosting the event to promote and support contributing to Open Source! The event is organized by a passionate group of open sorcerers and sponsored by ThoughtWorks.

Have more questions regarding Hacktoberfest? Head to the Hacktoberfest FAQ

Note: Participants are required to follow the Hacktoberfest Code Of Conduct

We love feedback! If you have any suggestions or cribs, feel free to fill out our feedback form. Don't worry, its completely anonymous.
